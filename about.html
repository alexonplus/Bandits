ABOUT

We started as a small group of enthusiasts passionate about comfortable, durable, high-quality urban hoodies. From the very beginning, we have always chosen to work with premium materials to ensure the longevity of our garments and the comfort and satisfaction of our customers.

Our clothing is designed to enhance urban culture, individuality, and freedom of expression, reflected in every detail. We believe in protecting the environment and source our materials from manufacturers committed to sustainable practices and responsible industrial processes.
